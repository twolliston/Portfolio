Train Station Application

In this portfolio, you have a train schedule application that incorporates Firebase to host arrival and departure data. This app retrieve and manipulate this information with Moment.js. This application will provide up-to-date information about various trains, namely their arrival times and how many minutes remain until they arrive at their station.
